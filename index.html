UAB School of Medicine

In 2013, after gathering information from our industry, we set out to redesign the UAB School of Medicine website with a user-focused navigation. We audited existing content and after a card sort and several focus groups we built wireframes and populated the content. We also added a phone tree and a steps-based application process to smooth the on-boarding process.

  • Client: UAB School of Medicine
  • Date: Jan 2013
  • Service: Web Development

UAB SOM News

The School of Medicine News site was developed to highlight news from around the school and to better communicate the events, accolades and breakthroughs that our hardworking medical faculty and staff developed. We tried to build categories that reflected the mission of the School of Medicine and to better facilitate communication among the departments.

  • Client: UAB School of Medicine
  • Date: Jan 2013
  • Service: Web Development

UAB SOM Departments

The SOM Department Sites were developed in collaboration with department faculty and staff and handed off to a staff member to maintain after the redesign was complete. Each department, clinic and center site had its own needs and challenges. We used analytics and department feedback to guide the elements that populated the homepages while trying to maintain a consistent navigational structure throughout all the sites.

  • Client: SOM Departments
  • Date: Ongoing
  • Service: Web Development

NBC13

The NBC13 redesigns were done for a variety of reasons. To increase user interaction and sharing on social networks, commenting was moved above the fold. We added additional ad positions to facilitate more face-time for our advertisers. After a content audit, weather was given more prominence on the page and in navigation. Related stories widgets were added to keep users on site longer. All of this after doing our due diligence in research and focus groups.

  • Client: NBC13
  • Date: Jan 2010
  • Service: Web Development

Campaign For UAB

The $1 Billion Campaign for UAB goals were to expand educational and cultural opportunities, advance research and patient care and fuel economic development. UAB has already reached over $500 million and 90% of that funding came from the School of Medicine. To increase giving, we designed a site for the development office to build awareness about how funding is used and to put donors in touch with our gifts officers. We modeled the site after the existing Campaign for UAB site to maintain consistency throughout.

  • Client: UAB SOM Development
  • Date: Jun 2014
  • Service: Web Development
